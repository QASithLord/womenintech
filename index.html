Seattle Women in Tech Events, Groups, and Resources

Created by Cyndy Eng-Dinsel

Having problems finding women in tech events? Looking to network, but have no idea where to start? You've come to the right place!

I've created this site to provide one place to go to for all your women in tech information. It will change as new events are posted and as my front end development skills improve. You can find some of my musings on women in tech events, struggles in the tech world, and in the kitchen on my new blog, http://qasithlord.blogspot.com.
