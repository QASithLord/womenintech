Seattle Women in Tech Events, Groups, and Resources

Having problems finding women in tech events? Looking to network, but have no idea where to start? You've come to the right place! Over the last few years I've found several groups and attended various meetups, and I've created this page to help you out. Like all sites, this is a work in progress. It will change as new events are posted and as my front end development skills improve. You can find some of my musings on women in tech events, struggles in the tech world, and in the kitchen on my new blog, http://qasithlord.blogspot.com

Resources

Free/Subsidized School/Programs/Classes/Tutorials

  • Ada Developers Academy. A training program for women and gender deverse people who want to become software developers. Located in Seattle.
  • LEAP Engineering Acceleration program is an immersive 16-week program sponsored by Microsoft.
  • Girl Develop It Seattle Chapter
  • Flexbox Zombies - Get better at Flexbox!
  • Udacity. Offer several programs that are free. If you want a certificate, then you need to spend money.
  • Stanford Online Computer Science 101

Other Resources

  • Lynda - Some public libraries have accounts you can use for free, as well as some companies offer this as a benefit.
  • Treehouse
  • Udemy - Pay per class. Often lots of discounts. Facebook has a group called Udemy Free Courses.
  • Ada Technical Books and Cafe. Cool events, books, and technical toys.
